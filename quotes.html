Episode List
Season 1

S01 E22 - Come on

Ted tries to stop Robin from going on a camping trip so that she can spend time with him, while Barney gets an old girlfriend to perform a rain dance.

S01 E21 - Milk

The dating agency finds Ted's perfect match but he decides not to meet her because he's still in love with Robin. Meanwhile, when Barney's prank war with the guy in the office opposite escalates, he enlists Marshall in the mischief-making.

S01 E20 - Best Prom Ever

Lily and Robin have to gatecrash a high school prom to check out the band because Marshall and Lily want to hire them for the wedding at short notice. Meanwhile, Robin finally forgives Ted.

S01 E19 - Mary the Paralegal

Robin takes Sandy, her fellow newscaster, as her date to an awards ceremony, and Barney hires a prostitute for Ted's date to make Robin jealous. At least, that's what he tells Ted.

S01 E18 - Nothing Good Happens After 2 Am

Ted wants to end his long-distance relationship with Victoria. While he's waiting for her to call him, convinced the relationship is over anyway, he finds out that Robin still has feelings for him. Being Ted, things end up getting worse instead of getting better.

S01 E17 - Life Among the Gorillas

Marshall begins his new job at Barney's law firm and quickly finds that Barney's colleagues are crude, obnoxious and laddish, mocking everything from his packed lunch to his fianc√©e. However, he doesn't want to give up, and learns to adapt.

S01 E16 - Cupcake

As Victoria gets offered a culinary scholarship in Germany, she and Ted have some big decisions to make.

S01 E15 - Game Night

Lily uncovers a video of Barney's and touches on his embarrassing past, but he'll only tell them the rest of the story if they'll share their own humiliating stories. Barney seizes the opportunity to get Ted to tell Victoria about his obsession with Robin.

S01 E14 - Zip, Zip, Zip

When Marshall and Lily decide not to go on their weekend break, Ted and Victoria head back to the flat to break their vow of abstinence in Ted and Marshall's living room.

S01 E13 - Drumroll Please

Having gone to Claudia and Stuart's wedding alone, Ted meets the girl of his dreams but doesn't get her name. He does everything he can to try and find her, but without her proper name it's beginning to look like he'll never see her again.

S01 E12 - The Wedding

Ted invites Robin to be his date at a friend's wedding, but it turns out he didn't request a guest place. Unfortunately, Robin is so excited he asked her that Ted can't bring himself to tell her she's not invited, so has to find a way to get her on the guest list.

S01 E11 - The Limo

It's New Year's Eve, and Ted hires a limo so they can all party together in style. Things don't go quite according to plan when all the parties they want to get to turn out to be useless.

S01 E10 - The Pineapple Incident

Ted goes on a boozy night out to try to stop himself from thinking. The next morning, he wakes up to find a stranger in his bed, but he can't remember a single thing about her.

S01 E09 - Belly Full of Turkey

Ted and Robin are surprised to bump into Barney when they volunteer at a homeless refuge during Thanksgiving. Lily's nerves are tested when she goes for Thanksgiving dinner at her future in-laws' house.

S01 E08 - The Duel

When Lily finally returns to her own flat, she discovers it's been turned into a Chinese restaurant and has to move in with Ted and Marshall. Barney convinces Ted that he's being eased out of the house and he must stand his ground.

S01 E07 - Matchmaker

Ted joins a dating agency with a remarkable success record in an attempt to meet the love of his life. Back at home, Marshall and Lily wage war with a household pest, which seems to be part mouse, part cockroach.

S01 E06 - The Slutty Pumpkin

Marshall and Lily enter a Halloween costume party and Robin brings her new boyfriend. Ted and Barney go to the same fancy dress party they go to every year where Ted waits for his lost love, the Slutty Pumpkin.

S01 E05 - Okay Awesome

Robin gets on the VIP list of a hot new club and can get the others in for free, but Lily has decided she and Marshall need to be more grown-up so she throws a wine and cheese evening with her teaching colleagues instead. Marshall busts out of the bathroom window and goes to the party with his friends.

S01 E04 - Return of the Shirt

Ted's views on life and love change when he re-evaluates a shirt he previously disliked. Meanwhile, Barney offers Robin money to say outlandish things live on air.

S01 E03 - The Sweet Taste of Liberty

Barney persuades Ted to join him picking up girls at JFK airport instead of going to the same boring bar as usual. When they find themselves on a plane to Philadelphia for the night, Ted wonders if he's made the right decision.

S01 E02 - Purple Giraffe

Ted is determined to win over Robin and since she doesn't want a serious relationship, decides to play it really cool. He casually invites her to a party at his place, but that means throwing a party especially, which is yet another distraction for Marshall who is trying to write a paper over the weekend.

S01 E01 - Pilot

When Robin must leave in the middle of their first date, Ted can hardly wait to see her again, and his eagerness threatens to scare her away.

Season 2

S02 E22 - Something Blue

Barney finds out that Ted and Robin split up two weeks before Marshall and Lily's wedding, but kept it secret so they wouldn't overshadow the event.

S02 E21 - Something Borrowed

When Marshall and Lily's wedding is not as they had envisioned it, Barney tries to salvage the special day.

S02 E20 - Showdown

It's two weeks before Lily and Marshall's wedding and the couple try to live apart to sort out the last-minute preparations. While at Robin's house, Lily discovers that she's lost too much weight to fit into her dress.

S02 E19 - Bachelor Party

Barney hires a stripper for Marshall's stag do, and Robin goes to Lily's bridal shower but finds her gift is out of place.

S02 E18 - Moving Day

Barney takes drastic action in order to keep Ted from moving in with Robin. Marshall and Lily discover the highs and lows of having the apartment to themselves.

S02 E17 - Arrivederci, Fiero

Marshall is ready to celebrate the 300,000th kilometre travelled by his car, a Fiero, but it stops right in the middle of the street.

S02 E16 - Stuff

Barney is a little too honest about Lily's performance in a terrible play. Meanwhile, Ted and Robin argue over what to do with the remnants of previous relationships.

S02 E15 - Lucky Penny

A string of bad luck ensues when Ted picks up a lucky penny; Marshall's injured toe keeps him out of the New York City Marathon.

S02 E14 - Monday Night Football

When a funeral foils Ted's Super Bowl plans, the gang makes a pact to remain in the dark about the winner until everyone can watch the recording together; star-struck Barney recognizes Emmitt Smith on the street.

S02 E13 - Columns

Ted finds it hard to fire his former boss, despite the abuse he receives from him. Meanwhile, Barney makes Lily a strange but tempting proposal.

S02 E12 - First Time in New York

The gang are prompted to think about their first times, as Robin tries desperately to stop her younger sister from losing her virginity.

S02 E11 - How Lily Stole Christmas

Lily and Ted fall out after Lily hears an old message on Marshall's machine encouraging Marshall to recall Lily's bad points, to help get over her, and ending in Ted calling Lily a grinch. Ted protests that he was trying to help his friend get over the break up as quickly as possible but Lily takes offence. Will the two ruin the gang's Christmas celebrations with their bickering?

S02 E10 - Single Stamina

Barney's gay brother (Wayne Brady) arrives as Barney is struggling with the realization that all his friends have settled into relationships.

S02 E09 - Slap Bet

Ted and Barney ponder women; Robin refuses to go to the mall; Robin covers up a secret from her past.

S02 E08 - Atlantic City

Marshall and Lily drag the gang to Atlantic City in order to elope. As they wait three days for the license, the gang gets into some mischief.

S02 E07 - Swarley

Lily gets a touch of the green eyes when Marshall goes on his first date after their split, and Marshall's date has her own ocular problem: `crazy eyes'.

S02 E06 - Aldrin Justice

Barney propositions Marshall's law professor (Jane Seymour); Lily tries to teach Ted's boss (Bryan Cranston) a lesson.

S02 E05 - World's Greatest Couple

Barney uses Lily to get rid of his one-night stand who won't go away, and newly-single Marshall realises how couple-orientated the world is.

S02 E04 - Ted Mosby, Architect

After Ted and Robin have their first big fight, Barney convinces Ted that being an architect is a great way of picking up ladies.

S02 E03 - Brunch

Ted's family's visit to New York leads to the discovery of a family secret; Lily and Marshall struggle to get used to their new relationship.

S02 E02 - The Scorpion and the Toad

Barney helps Marshall to work on his bachelor skills. Robin helps Lily find a new apartment on her return from San Francisco, but soon tires of her endless stories.

S02 E01 - Where Were We

The gang tries to take Marshall's mind off his break up with Lily. This isn't helped by Marshall finding a credit card bill with some of her charges listed.
